Our history

When Facebook launched in 2004, it changed the way that people connect. Apps such as Messenger, Instagram and WhatsApp further empowered billions around the world. Now, Meta has moved beyond 2D screens towards immersive experiences in mixed reality and innovation with AI to help build the next evolution in social technology.
